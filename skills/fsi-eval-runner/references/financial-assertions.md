# Financial Assertions Library

Reusable assertions organized by category. Copy and adapt for specific skill evals.

## Balance & Integrity

- "Balance sheet balances: total assets equal total liabilities plus total equity"
- "Income statement flows: revenue minus all operating and non-operating expenses equals net income"
- "Cash flow statement ties: ending cash equals beginning cash plus net cash from operations, investing, and financing"
- "Retained earnings roll forward: beginning RE + net income - dividends = ending RE"
- "Sources & uses balance: total sources equal total uses in an LBO"
- "Debt schedule ties: beginning debt + draws - repayments = ending debt"

## Valuation Reasonableness

- "WACC is between 5% and 20%"
- "Terminal growth rate is between 0% and 4%"
- "Terminal growth rate does not exceed the risk-free rate"
- "EV/EBITDA multiples are between 3x and 50x for non-distressed companies"
- "P/E ratios are between 5x and 100x"
- "LBO entry multiple is within 2x of peer median EV/EBITDA"
- "IRR for the base case LBO is between 10% and 40%"
- "Implied share price from DCF is within 50% of current market price"
- "Football field shows min/median/max from at least 3 methodologies"

## Comps & Precedents

- "Comparable company table includes at least 5 public peers"
- "All peers are in the same or adjacent SIC/GICS sector as the target"
- "Median multiples are calculated and displayed"
- "Outliers (>2 standard deviations) are flagged or footnoted"
- "Precedent transaction table includes at least 3 deals"
- "All precedent deals occurred within the last 5 years"
- "Transaction premiums are calculated where pre-announcement prices available"

## Data Provenance

- "Every financial data point cites its source (MCP provider name or filing reference)"
- "As-of dates are displayed for all market data"
- "Unsourced or estimated data is flagged as [UNSOURCED] or [ESTIMATED]"
- "MCP provider names match the actual providers used (no phantom citations)"

## Regulatory & Compliance

- "Output includes forward-looking statement disclaimer (for client-facing materials)"
- "Research note includes Reg AC analyst certification language"
- "Research note includes conflict-of-interest disclosures"
- "KYC output includes AML/BSA compliance language"
- "Client materials include confidential watermark"

## Output Format

- "Excel model uses correct color coding: blue for inputs, black for formulas, green for links"
- "All assumption cells have named ranges"
- "Balance check row exists and shows zero"
- "Print areas are set for all key tabs"
- "PowerPoint includes page numbers on every slide"
- "All data slides have source footnotes"

## Performance Metrics

- "Skill completes in under 5 minutes (300 seconds) for a standard prompt"
- "Token usage is under 50,000 for a standard evaluation"
- "No more than 30 tool calls for a single eval prompt"
